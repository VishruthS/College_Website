Admission

Undergradute Admission Process

Affiliated to University of Mysore (UOM). Admission is open to students who have passed any one of the following:

  • 10+2 examination of PU / CBSE with English as a subject of study.
  • 3 years of Diploma. Minimum of 50 percent marks in the final examination.
  • Admission is made according to the directives of UOM and Karnataka Government as applicable to each academic year.

Fee Structure

Tuition fee as fixed by the University of Mysore for the academic year 2020-21 (tentative) is as mentioned below:

  • Tuition fee for B. E. admitted through CET quota: Rs.58,806/- per year
  • Tuition fee for B. E. admitted through COMED-K / Management(*) quota:
    • For CSE, CSE(AI & ML) & ISE: Rs. 2,00,000/- per year
    • For ME & EC: Rs. 1,50,000/- per year
    • For CIV: Rs. 1,50,000/- per year
    • Other fees/University/Exams fees payable at the time of admission for the academic year 2020-21 is Rs. 24,500/- per year
